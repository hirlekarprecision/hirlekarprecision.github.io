--- category: Filter Monitoring language: en title: Irrigation Filtration image: irrigation-filtration.jpg description: Used to moniter filter clogging in the irrigation industry. advantages: Cost effective method to moniter clogged filter. note: | Water from canals is filtered before it is used for irrigation. The Hirlekar Precision DGC 320 detects when the filter is clogged and gives out a signal using a switch. At this point, back flush starts which helps remove and drain the sediments from the filter thereby ensuring that unfiltered water does not get get supplied to farms. ---
